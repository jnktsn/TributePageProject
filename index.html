The Chilling Predictions of

Nostradamus

The Controversial Seer Just May Have Gotten a Few Things Right...

Michel de Nostredame -latinised as Nostradamus-, sixteenth century french physician, author, and seer.

Coincidence or Prophesy?

Here are five times Nostradamus’ predictions may have come true:

  1. "The penultimate of the surname of Prophet will take Diana [Thursday] for his day and rest: He will wander because of a frantic head, And delivering a great people from subjection." -Nostradamus
    • ...a princess died?

      • Many people believe this prophecy refers to the 1997 death of Princess Diana.

  2. "He will come to travel to the corner of Luna, Where he will be captured and put in a strange land, The unripe fruits to be subject of great scandal, Great blame, to one, great praise." -Nostradamus
    • ...man walked on the moon?

      • With its reference to ‘Luna’, Nostradamus scholars believe this is obviously a prediction of the historic 1969 Moon landing.

  3. "The blood of the just will be demanded of London Burnt by fire in the year ’66 The ancient Lady will fall from her high place and many of the same sect will be killed." -Nostradamus
    • ...an iconic city burned?

      • With the reference to London burning and the year ’66, many people believe this prophesy is undoubtedly a prediction of the Great London Fire of 1666.

  4. "PAU, NAY, LORON will be more of fire than of the blood, To swim in praise, the great one to flee to the confluence. He will refuse entry to the Piuses, The depraved ones and the Durance will keep them imprisoned." -Nostradamus
    • ...a man became an emperor?

      • Creatively unscrambling the words ‘PAU NAY LORON’, true believers claim, proves Nostradamus foresaw the rise of french monarch Napoleon Bonaparte.

  5. "Near the gates and within two cities there will be scourges the like of which was never seen, Famine within plague, people put out by steel, Crying to the great immortal God for relief." -Nostradamus
    • ...mankind unleashed the power of the atom?

      • To the Nostradamus faithful, this prediction can only be foretelling one thing; the dropping of atomic bombs on the cities of Hiroshima and Nagasaki in 1945.

Could Nostradamus see the future, or have the meanings of hispredictionsbeen wishfully applied after the fact? We may never know.

Whatever the truth, one fact is certain:
Nostradamus was a fascinating person.
